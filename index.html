About Us

NusaCoco Charcoal is your reliable partner in sourcing premium coconut shell charcoal from Indonesia. We focus on supplying consistent, export-ready charcoal products that are carefully produced from selected coconut shells across the Indonesian archipelago. By combining strict quality control with professional handling, we ensure every shipment meets the expectations of international buyers.

Built on the vision of bringing Indonesia’s coconut-based energy to the world, NusaCoco Charcoal emphasizes sustainability, product reliability, and long-term partnerships. We work closely with local producers, implement responsible production practices, and prioritize clear communication so our clients can confidently grow their shisha, BBQ, or private label brands with a stable charcoal supply.
