Shisa Charcoal Briquette

Premium coconut shell cube briquettes designed for long-lasting, clean heat—perfect for shisha.

Product Overview

Our shisha charcoal briquettes are made from carefully selected coconut shells and pressed into uniform cubes specifically for hookah use. Each briquette is engineered to deliver a long, steady burn with minimal smoke so guests can enjoy the flavour of their shisha without distraction.

Thanks to their consistent size and density, the briquettes are easy to light, simple to rotate on the bowl, and give lounge operators predictable heat control throughout every session.

Key Features & Benefits

Optimized for Hookah Heat Management – Cube shape and controlled density provide even heat distribution across the bowl, helping to reduce hot spots and burnt tobacco.

Clean-Tasting Sessions – Neutral aroma and very low volatile content mean the charcoal will not overpower or change the flavour profile of the shisha mix.

Minimal Residue After Use – Designed to leave a compact, fine ash layer that is easy to remove between sessions, keeping bowls and trays cleaner for staff.

Long-Lasting Burn Time – Extended burn profile helps reduce the number of coal changes per guest, saving time and labour in busy lounges.

Low Spark & Crackle – Carefully controlled production conditions reduce popping and sparking, creating a calmer and more premium guest experience.

Typical Applications

  • Shisha and hookah lounges
  • Home shisha users
  • Café and restaurant use
  • Private label brands for shisha charcoal
