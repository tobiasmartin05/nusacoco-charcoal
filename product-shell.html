Raw Coconut Shell Charcoal

Natural Raw Coconut Shell Charcoal crafted for strong, clean heat and an authentic live-fire experience—ideal for traditional grilling, wood-fired concepts, and premium BBQ.

Product Overview

Our raw coconut shell charcoal is produced as a stable, export-ready material for manufacturers and industrial users who require a reliable charcoal source. The charcoal is processed to specific moisture and fixed-carbon ranges, then screened into controlled size fractions, making it easier for clients to integrate into their own production lines. By starting with consistent raw material, briquette factories and other users can maintain product quality while reducing time spent on sorting and pre-treatment.

Key Features & Benefits

Manufacturing-Grade Specification – IMoisture, ash, and fixed carbon levels are managed within agreed ranges to support predictable performance in downstream processes.

Screened Size Options – Charcoal can be supplied in selected size grades (lump or crushed), helping reduce additional crushing or sieving work at the client’s facility.

Good Mechanical Strength – Proper carbonisation and handling produce charcoal that resists excessive breakage during transport and unloading.

Flexible Packaging Formats – Available in bulk bags or other packing options suitable for containerised export and warehouse storage.

Sustainable Coconut Feedstock – Sourced from coconut shells, making it an attractive alternative to wood charcoal for companies seeking more responsible raw materials.

Typical Applications

  • Feedstock for charcoal briquette factories
  • Industrial users requiring charcoal for boilers, kilns, or heat treatment
  • Blending material for metallurgical or foundry applications (where suitable specs are required)
  • Trading and distribution as raw charcoal to regional markets
