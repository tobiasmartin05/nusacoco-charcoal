About Us

NusaCoco Charcoal is a trading and export company focused on supplying premium coconut shell charcoal products from Indonesia to the world. We connect carefully selected local producers with international buyers, providing coconut shell briquettes and lump charcoal that support cleaner, more sustainable energy use.

Founded with the aim of turning Indonesia’s abundant coconut resources into high-value products for global markets, NusaCoco Charcoal places strong emphasis on reliability, transparency, and long-term partnerships. Whether you are a shisha brand, BBQ distributor, or industrial user, our role is to make your charcoal sourcing simpler, safer, and more consistent.

Our Core Principles

Our day-to-day operations are guided by four core principles:

Responsible Sourcing

We work with trusted Indonesian producers who use 100% coconut shells as raw material. By utilizing agricultural by-products, we help reduce waste and support more sustainable alternatives to traditional wood charcoal.

Consistent Quality

Every shipment is selected based on key export parameters such as fixed carbon, moisture, ash content, size, and shape consistency. This focus on quality ensures stable heat, low smoke, and clean performance for your end customers.

Reliable Logistics

From supplier coordination to loading supervision and export documentation, we aim to make the logistics side as smooth as possible. Our goal is simple: predictable shipments so you can focus on growing your market.

Partnership Mindset

We don’t see ourselves as just a supplier, but as a partner in your supply chain. Open communication, honest feedback, and flexibility as your volume grows are at the center of how we work with every client.

Vision

To be a globally trusted partner for coconut shell charcoal from Indonesia—delivering reliable, sustainable products that support businesses, respect the environment, and showcase the strength of Indonesia’s natural resources.

Mission

Deliver Reliable Products
To supply export-ready coconut shell briquettes and lump charcoal with consistent quality that meets diverse market requirements.

Promote Sustainable Energy
To prioritize coconut shell–based charcoal as a cleaner, more responsible fuel option compared to traditional wood-based alternatives.

Support Local Communities
To collaborate with Indonesian producers, farmers, and processors, helping create stable demand and long-term economic opportunities at the source.

Improve Continuously
To keep refining our sourcing, quality control, and logistics processes so clients experience smoother transactions and better product performance over time.

Expand Global Connections
To build enduring relationships with buyers across regions—Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and beyond—through transparent service and dependable supply.
